How introductions happen

Introductions are free when matched with a paying member. If two non-member profiles are highly compatible, our flexible Second Match Promise membership applies - only one person needs to join. If your first introduction is not the right fit, your membership continues with another carefully selected introduction - providing a more thoughtful and reassuring approach to modern matchmaking.

Free Where Eligible · Second Match Promise

*Second Match Promise - £150. A flexible membership that includes a guaranteed second introduction if your first match isn't the right fit. No fixed time limit.
