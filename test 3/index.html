Founder

"I built the community
I couldn't find."

Fifteen years in health data and enterprise software. A career built around ambitious people - and a social life that, if I'm honest, thinned out faster than I expected.

For over ten years, dating apps were my default - I never had the time to go out, and as the years went by my social circle quietly shrank. Friends paired off and moved on with their partners, and the single, like-minded people left in my own world became almost nonexistent.

What I actually craved was a community - somewhere to keep learning, exploring, and socialising, with a real chance of naturally meeting someone built into it, not forced. Finding that on my own would have been its own time-consuming search. Having someone speed up that process and match me with people equally ready to meet someone like-minded would have been the perfect solution - except I could never find a community like that.

I wanted a room full of people who understood the ambition and the loneliness that can come with it - some to be friends with, some to learn from, and yes, some I might fall for.

So I became an ABIA-registered matchmaker and built it myself.

- Anna, Founder
